My Narrative

From Medical Doctor to Creative Catalyst

2018

The Traditional Path

As a clinical medicine student and doctor, my world was defined by wards, shifts, and diagnostics. While fulfilling, I felt a deep calling toward a creative life. I began writing online, sharing lessons on mental clarity and cognitive efficiency under high-stress hospital conditions.

2020

The Clean Break

I made the terrifying choice to leave medicine. With no guarantee of success, I launched my YouTube channel and digital newsletter. By treating creative work with the same rigorous diagnostic methodology I learned in medicine, my audience scaled to 100K in under a year.

2023-2026

Bestselling Author & Speaker

I published the bestselling journal Find Your Focus and built a multi-channel media engine. I now partner with premier brands that respect user focus. We deliver storytelling that respects our readers' intellect and drives massive, authentic conversions.
